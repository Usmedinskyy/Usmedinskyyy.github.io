How to Choose the Right Stairlift

Gamburd

Now that you’ve decided that a stairlift is the best option for you, how do you choose the best one for your needs?

We recommend that you first start by discussing this with our in house consultant. Based on your specific mobility issues, they’ll be able to advise you what the best choice might be. There’s also the option of testing out various stairlift models before making your purchase. We can provide demonstrations to prospective customers at any of our locations. Perhaps most importantly, properly assess the staircase in which you’ll be installing the stairlift.

At Gamburd, we are honored to offer the best prices on stairlifts in the Southern California area.

We are glad to answer any questions you have about each of our stairlift models and provide an assessment as to what would be the best fit for you.

Some of the questions you should be prepared to answer include:

  • Are you able to bend your knees and comfortably remain seated?
  • Do you prefer to travel in a seated or standing position?
  • Are you able to comfortably work a stairlift remote control?

When we come to your home to assess your needs, we take measurements of the stairs and leave you with the most competitive quote in the industry.

All of our quotes are free with no obligation to buy and our products come with a lifetime warranty. After our initial assessment, you will be provided with a written quotation for the entire cost ofthe stairlift, including the labor for installation and shipping. During our visit, we can also explain after-installation services, how to utilize your warranty and what to do if your stairlift becomes damaged or broken. From that point, we leave you to make your decision independently. But we trust that you will choose Gamburd.
